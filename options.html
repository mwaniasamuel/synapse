About Synapse

Cognitive Learning Acceleration Platform

Synapse is a Chrome extension that adapts to your cognitive patterns in real-time, optimizing content presentation and building personalized knowledge maps to accelerate your learning journey.

Key Features

  • Real-time cognitive state detection
  • Adaptive content presentation
  • Personal knowledge mapping
  • Learning session management
  • Privacy-first design

Research Foundation

Built on cognitive science research addressing the challenges of digital learning in an AI-dominated world. Designed specifically to enhance focus and knowledge retention for African learners and global digital education.
